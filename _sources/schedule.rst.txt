- **3 Oct (Thu)**: A tour through available components and Arduino Uno
- **15 Oct (Tue)**: Lecture on design approaches
- **22 Oct (Tue)**: Training on 3D printing with Techlab (TBD)
- **7 Nov (Thu)**: Spectrophotometer demo day
- **28 Nov (Thu)**: Thanksgiving Day: no class
- **5 Dec (Thu)**: Pulse oximetry demo day
- **12 Dec (Thu)**: Tear down and clean up
