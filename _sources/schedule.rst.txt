Weekly activities
-----------------

In each lab session, which are Tuesdays and Thursdays, 10:30-11:55 am, you will be working on your exercises or projects. Dates with special activities are shown below with schedule changes possible and possibly more to be announced as the term progresses.

- **29 Sept (Tue)**: Welcome, safety, introduction to Arduino
- **7-28 Oct**: Training on 3D printing with Techlab (on your own)
- **14 Oct (Tue)**: Lecture on CAD for 3D printing (optional)
- **21 Oct (Tue)**: Lecture on EE basics (optional)
- **28 Oct (Tue)**: Spectrophotometer project introduction
- **6 Nov (Thu)**: Spectrophotometer demo day
- **11 Nov (Tue)**: Pulse oximetry project introduction
- **27 Nov (Thu)**: Thanksgiving Day: no class
- **4 Dec (Thu)**: Pulse oximetry demo day
- **11 Dec (Thu)**: Tear down and clean up
